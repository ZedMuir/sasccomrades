Meet the HQ Team

Who are the staff in HQ SASC?

Lieutenant Colonel Lee Jenkins - Commanding Officer

Lt Col Lee Jenkins joined the Army in Aug 1986, training as a Junior soldier at the newly opened Light Division Depot Winchester, on completion of training he joined the 1st Bn Royal Green Jackets in Osnabruck as part of the British Army of the Rhine. He completed several tours of Northern Ireland and Belize as well as deploying on exercises in Germany, Cyprus, Canada, Denmark and Norway. He transferred to the Small Arms School Corps in Jan 2000 from the Gunnery School Lulworth.
On successful completion of SASC candidate selection, he was posted back to the Gunnery School as a school’s instructor within the Infantry Wing. He moved again to Training Advisory Group (TAG) Germany in August 2002 where he was a SA advisor and deployed on Op TELIC. In Aug 2003 he promoted to Warrant Officer Class Two and was posted to the Black Watch in Fallingbostel. He took up position as the Battalion Gunnery Warrant Officer, where he oversaw the Highlanders technical conversion from Light to Armoured Infantry. He exercised with the battalion in BATUS and Poland before deploying on Op TELIC 5 as Ops WO for Tac Bravo in Iraq. On his return from Iraq he returned to Lulworth as the Wing Sergeant Major of the Inf Wing. He was promoted to Warrant Officer Class One in 2009 and formed the Operational Shooting Trg Team responsible for the introduction of CQM into the British Army.
He commissioned in July 2011 and spend two years as the Training Officer at ATR Winchester prior to moving for the last time to Lulworth as the Training Development Officer. In August 2015 he moved to Germany and took up the post of SO3 G7 within HQ 20 Armd Inf Bde. During this time, he oversaw the trg for 20X to take over the role of Very High Readiness Joint taskforce (VJTF) and their initial deployment to EFP in Estonia.
2017 saw him promoted to major and moving to take up the role as SO2 G7 Trg and Policy within ITG. In Sep 2018 he moved to Royal Military Academy Sandhurst as Chief Instructor of the Dismounted Close Combat Wing. In February 2021 he was selected for promotion to Lieutenant Colonel and appointed the Commanding Officer Small Arms School Corps early in Jan 2022.
Lt Col Jenkins has been married to Debbie for 26 years. They have three boys, Daniel, Stephen and Ryan. They are also grandparents to Evie, Ava Rose, Oscar and Luna with a fifth grandchild due to grace them with their presence in the summer of 22.
As well as being a dad and grandad, he enjoys reading, travel and any sort of Physical exercise.
